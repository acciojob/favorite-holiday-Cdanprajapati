Paris - The City of Lights


Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture. From iconic landmarks to world-class cuisine, this city offers an unforgettable experience for travelers.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most famous landmark, offering breathtaking city views.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-renowned Mona Lisa and other masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture with a rich history.
  4. Seine River Cruise – A romantic way to explore Paris at night.
  5. Champs-Élysées & Arc de Triomphe – A perfect spot for shopping and sightseeing.

Must-Try Foods

  • French Croissant – A buttery, flaky pastry perfect for breakfast.
  • Escargot – A traditional delicacy made from cooked snails with garlic butter.
  • Ratatouille – A delicious vegetable stew, famous worldwide.
  • Macarons – Sweet, colorful treats that melt in your mouth.
  • French Onion Soup – A heartwarming soup topped with melted cheese.
